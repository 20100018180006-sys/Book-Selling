About DrukPages

A platform born from love for Bhutanese literature, storytelling, and preserving our culture through books.

Why I Made This Website

I made this website because I wanted to create a simple and convenient platform for Bhutanese people to buy, sell, and publish books.

Living in Bhutan, I saw that many people face difficulty finding good Bhutanese books. At the same time, many talented local writers don’t have an easy way to share their stories with readers.

So I created DrukPages as a place where:

  • You can buy new and used Bhutanese books
  • You can resell old books to other readers
  • New writers can publish their books online
  • Bhutanese stories can reach more people

This website is made with love for our culture, our stories, and our people.

Our Mission

To preserve Bhutanese literature and make books accessible to every Bhutanese reader. We aim to empower local authors by providing a platform where they can publish and connect with readers across the country.

DrukPages is more than just a bookstore. It is a movement to celebrate Bhutanese stories, strengthen literary heritage, and connect book lovers throughout Bhutan.
